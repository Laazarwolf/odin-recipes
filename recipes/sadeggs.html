Sad Eggs

So sad to be all alone in the world

This is possibly the worst way to prepare eggs. There really isn't any way to do less and still call it a recipe. I mean I guess you could just put some eggs in a bowl without cracking them... but that would just be lazy eggs, not sad.

Consumption Warning!

Ingredients

    Eggs:

  • 2 Eggs of any egg-laying creature, sadness optional, it can be added later

Directions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl
  2. Add sadness
